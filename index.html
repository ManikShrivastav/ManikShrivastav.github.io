Best Education

Shree Nrisingh Madhyamik Vidyalaya is an educational institute that offers quality education to its students. It is a community school which not only gives education but also participates in various programmes and make students practical.

Our Achievements

Our Students not only study but they also participate in various programmes. Our students have participated in various programmes like IT FAIR OF BIRGUNJ 2076, ICT Awards Nepal 2078, Winter Hardware Wonderland 2079. Our Students have participated in international programmes like Winter Hardware Wonderland organized by Hack Club from California,USA. We offer workshop for the projects of students and our highly qualified teachers guide them. We are the hub for engineering students in Birgunj.

Our Faculties

We have Highly Qualified faculty memebers and teachers. Our Teachers are highly trained and highly skilled in their work of teaching. Apart from teaching, our faculties have trained and experienced professionals who have worked in different industries and serve here to give experience to our students. Our Students are very lucky to have teachers and faculties like this.

Choose Your Course

BLE Class 8

We offer classes from class 1 to class 8 according to the syllabus of National Examination Board,Nepal. It is also known as Basic Level Examination. These are the basic classes of education.

NEB Class 12

We offer classes secondary school courses in different faculties from class 11 to class 12 as per National Examination Board,Nepal..

NEB Class 10

We offer classes from class 1 to class 10 according to the syllabus of National Examination Board,Nepal. These are the basic classes of education.

Technical Education and Vocational Training

We offer Technical Education and Vocational Training which runs under National Examination Board, Government of Nepal. This course specifies in Computer Engineering. It is similar to Diploma in Computer Engineering according to Nepal's Government. Our Technical students have participated in different programmes including an international project.

Vocational Training

OJT also known as Over the Job Training are the compulsory elecments in the technical education of Computer Education. It is the vocational training given to students to make them job ready.
